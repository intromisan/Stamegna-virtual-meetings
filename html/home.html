More meetings = more deals

The more people you meet, the more deals you'll close. We will organise one-to-one scheduled online meetings to match your needs.

Find your business partners

Stamegna Virtual Meetings was launched to meet the demand of finding your right business partners in this unprecedented times. Still the same service and upmost attention to detail but in a different format where you can meet your business partners from the comfort of your office.

For sellers...

Since the past 10 years, Stamegna has been organising business meetings all over the world. Our database consists of thousand contacts and we will find the right buyers for your category of products.

For buyers...

Stamegna Retail Management Introduces Stamegna Virtual Meetings where you can negotiate with your category - matching prospective suppliers during a session of one-to-one online meetings.

Pre-schedule the meetings

After registration we will schedule and send you the agenda for your match-made meetings. During our 15 minutes one-to-one online meetings in a series of up to 8 category-specific negotiations, you will meet your potential partners, without need of travelling!

Price

1990 Euro to include pre scheduled one-to-one meetings with carefully selected category buyers.
An account manager will work with each company to help them prepare for the meeting, collect the necessary information and discuss the schedule.

Support during the event

Our Account Managers will moderate the meetings and provide live support during them if you need any assistance. After the meeting series, you will receive your partners' data from Stamegna Management team.
